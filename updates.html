Updates

I keep tweaking the games to make them more fun to play.
Here's a log of the updates so far — new bosses, sound effects, balance changes, UI improvements, and more.

July 4, 2026 New Game Cthulhu Typing

Cthulhu Typing is out!

A typing-meets-summoning battle: every word you finish typing summons a soldier that auto-attacks a daily boss from the Cthulhu Mythos (43 in all). It has a leaderboard that resets each day, and a run takes just 2–3 minutes, once a day. Play →

June 26, 2026 New Game Piano Typing

Piano Typing is out!

A rhythm-meets-typing game: falling tiles show you the next key to press, and playing at your own pace lets you perform classic melodies. There's no timing judgment, so it also makes for great home-row practice. Play →

June 24, 2026 New Bosses Mythos Slayer

Added 30 new bosses — now 40 in all!

Dropped in 30 more fearsome enemies at once, bringing the total number of bosses to beat up to 40. There's a lot more to sink your teeth into now — take down bosses you haven't seen yet and keep growing your army stronger. Play →

June 23, 2026 Balance Mythos Slayer

Prestige bonus bumped up to 5x

The power-up multiplier you get from prestiging is now 5x. Every reset makes you snap back to strength much faster, and idle earnings got a boost too.

June 22, 2026 Update Mythos Slayer

Made the HP bar easier to read

Reworked the boss HP bar interface so you can tell at a glance how much further you have to go before the boss goes down.

June 22, 2026 Update Popcorn Typing

Added a completion sound for extra satisfaction

Added a sound effect for finishing a word all the way through. Nailing that final key feels even crisper now, making the whole thing more satisfying to play. Play →

June 21, 2026 New Game Mythos Slayer
