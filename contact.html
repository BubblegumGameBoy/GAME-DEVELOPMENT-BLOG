Contact

Got a bug report, feedback, or a work inquiry? Use the form below. Even a quick "I loved this part" is very welcome — I (Bubblegum) read every message that comes in. Replies usually within a few days.

Before You Write

Here's the kind of thing I'm happy to hear about. Depending on your question, checking the relevant page first may get you an answer faster.

  • Bug reports — "It won't load," "my save disappeared," and the like. It helps a lot if you mention your device (PC/phone), your browser, and which game and moment the problem happened in — that makes tracking down the cause much quicker.
  • Feedback and requests — features you'd like added, anything that felt awkward to play. I use it to guide future updates.
  • Work inquiries — requests or questions about game development, writing, and similar. Please include a short summary and how to reach you.
  • About site content — corrections to articles, notes about assets or rights, and so on.

For how-to-play and strategy questions, each game page has a "How to Play" and "FAQ" section, and there are strategy write-ups over on the blog. Have a look there too.

Response Times

Since I run this on my own, replies can take anywhere from a few days to about a week. I may not be able to respond to everything, but I do read every message. Bug reports get worked into fixes over time whether or not I reply directly.

Any information you enter in the form is used only to handle your inquiry. See the Privacy Policy for details.
